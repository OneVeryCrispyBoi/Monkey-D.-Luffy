Monkey D. Luffy

Who is he?

Monkey D. Luffy was born and raised in a small village in the East Blue. He is the son of the numbver one revalutionary. "Monkey D. Dragon" and the grandson of the legendary hero of the navy, "Monkey D. Garp". The orgins of Luffy's mother and grandmother are unknown. His dream is to become the greatest pirate in the world. He began his journey at the age of 17.

Monkey D. Dragon(revolutionary)

Monkey D. Dragon is the father of Monkey D. Luffy. Very little is known about him. He is the leader of the revolution which makes him a very dangerous man to the navy and the world government.

Monkey D. Garp(Hero of the Navy)

Monkey D. Garp is the grandfather of Monkey D. Luffy. He is a Vice-Admiral in the navy ranks and refuses to go up in the ranks because it would mean that he would have more responsibilities and less freedom. He earned the title of "Hero of the Navy" when he was able to stop a force that threatend the entire world with the help of the king of the pirates, Gol D. Roger. They stopped that force at an island that was known as "God Valley". The fight of this threat ended with the destruction of the whole island. That event woulkd later be called "the God Valley incident".

what is Luffy's Goal?

Luffy's goal is to sail acorss the earth's sea to find the greatest treasure in the world, the One Piece. Wealth, fame, power, everything a man could ask for made up all into one treasure. Luffy plans to sail out with his crew to find the one piece and become "King of the Pirates"

His Past

Growing up his grandpa trained him everyday with hard tasks to become a navy soldier anmd protect people from pirates. One day he met "Red-Haired Shanks", a pirate. At first, Luffy wanted to fight them but then he began to realise that pirates were able to do anything they wanted with no consequences whatsoever. He saw that the pirates were having an amazing time partying, eating, drinking, and singing. Luffy had realized how much freedom the pirates had and then he had made his mind. He was going to become the king of the pirates and become the person with the most freedom in the world.

The World of One Piece

The world is dived into 4 powers;Pirates, the navy, the revolution, and the world government. The world government sits at the top of the world's power. many people do not dare to defy the world government but pirates could care less. The navy serves under the world government. The navy protects everyday citizens of the world from corrupt people and pirates. Pirates plunder and loot other pirates they rebel against the navy and do whatever they feel like doing. Pirates like having the freedom to do whatever they want. The revolution works from the shadows and the oppose the world government directly. They plan to take down the world government which holds most of the power of the world amnd create a new and just world.
